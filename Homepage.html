ABOUT US

Naisula School - Tilisi is a new school offering the British National Curriculum from Pre Primary, Primary to Lower secondary. The School opened on 8th of January 2024 and admissions are ongoing. We are located within Tilisi Development, near Limuru in close proximity to major highways, making us readily accessible from Northern, Western and Southern Nairobi and metropolitan hubs in these areas.

VISION

To be a model school that offers learning in a stimulating, friendly and multicultural environment where each learner is respected, nurtured and inspired to achieve the highest standards of intellectual and personal development.

MISSION

Develop young men and women of high integrity who are resilient and adaptable; with a disposition to continue with higher education and become socially responsible citizens of the world.

FEES STRUCTURE

The annual fee is KES 900,000. Year 8: Students in Year 8 pay KES 307,300 per term or KES 921,900 per year. Year 9, 10, and 11: The school fee per term for Years 9, 10, and 11 is KES 314,200 or KES 942,600 per year. However, parents can pay the fee in two equal instalments once a student is in Year 11
